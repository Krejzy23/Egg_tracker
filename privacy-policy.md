# Privacy Policy – Egg Tracker

Last updated: 2026

Egg Tracker ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

**The app may collect the following information:**

- Email address (used for authentication)

- Egg production records entered by the user

- Technical information required for app functionality

## How We Use Information

**The collected information is used only to:**

- allow users to sign in to their account

- store egg production records

- provide statistics and reminders

- improve the functionality of the app

## Data Storage

User data is stored securely using Firebase services provided by Google.

## Notifications

The app may send optional reminder notifications to help users remember to record egg production. Notifications can be disabled in the app settings.

## Data Sharing

The app does **not sell or share personal data with third parties.**

Firebase services may process data as part of providing authentication and data storage.

## Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting the developer.
